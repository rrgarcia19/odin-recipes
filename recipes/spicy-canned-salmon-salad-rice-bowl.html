Spicy Canned Salmon Salad Rice Bowl

Description

Canned salmon mixed with rice vinegar, Sriracha, and mayonnaise makes this one spicy salmon salad rice bowl. Fresh cucumbers, quickly marinated in soy sauce, and sliced avocado add some coolness.

Feel free to make this bowl your own by adding more ingredients.

This delicious spicy canned salmon salad rice bowl recipe takes 15 minutes of prep time yielding 2 servings.

Nutrition facts (per serving): 908 Calories, 58g Fat, 46g Carbs, 54g Protein

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped English cucumber
  • 1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 (14 3/4 ounce) can pink salmon, sking and bone removed, drained and flaked
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons chile-garlic sauce (such as Siracha)
  • 1 tablespoon light mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onion for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine chopped cucumbers and soy sauce in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the salmon, Sriracha, mayonnaise, and rice vinegar.
  3. In a bowl place cooked rice and top it with the salmon mixture, sliced avocado, and chopped cucumber mixture. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Top with extra Sriracha and green onions if desired.
